<product_requirements>
The project is to build a comprehensive, commercial-ready mobile application named ThaiTeach for both iOS and Android, aimed at teaching the Thai language to users of all levels.

**Core Learning Content:**
The app must contain a vast library of over 575 learning items, including:
- Thai alphabet and numbers (1-1,000,000).
- Extensive vocabulary categories: animals, human anatomy, clothing, emotions, household items, etc.
- Basic to intermediate conversational phrases.
- Grammar, sentence patterns, pronouns, and verbs.
- Copyright-free educational songs with lyrics.

**Key Features:**
- **Interactive Learning:** A flashcard-style interface with a tap to reveal function.
- **Audio Pronunciation:** Text-to-Speech (TTS) for all Thai words, which triggers automatically when a card's answer is revealed.
- **UI/UX:** A modern, stylish, and professional user interface featuring icons, gradients, and custom-styled tabs. Includes a global dark/light mode.
- **Personalization:** Users can favorite items and track their progress.
- **Authentication:** Secure user registration and login (including social sign-in with Google/Facebook) with two-factor authentication and an automated account recovery process.
- **Monetization:** A freemium model with a premium subscription (£5.99/month) unlocking advanced content, managed via Google Play Store.
- **Internationalization (i18n):** Multi-language support with translations for a global audience.
- **Branding:** A dedicated About section with credits to Jake Adamson and a support email.
